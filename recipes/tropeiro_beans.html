Tropeiro Beans

Description

Tropeiro beans are a traditional and tasty dish in Brazilian cuisine. Prepared with ingredients such as bacon, calabrian sausage, olive oil, onion, garlic, cooked beans, eggs, manioc flour and salt. This dish is a unique combination of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 150g of chopped bacon
  • 1 cup of olive oil
  • 6 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 bacon stock cube
  • 250g of calabrian sausage
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 500g of cooked and drained beans
  • 4 fried and chopped eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 spoonful of manioc fluor

Steps

  1. Put the oil in a pan and fry the bacon and sausage separately, then set aside;
  2. In the same pan, add the salt, fry the onion and garlic, add the drained beans and the bacon stock;
  3. Add the bacon and sausage, the manioc fluor and the chopped fried eggs.
