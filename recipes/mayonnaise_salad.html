Mayonnaise Salad

Description

This is a recipe for a mixture of different vegetables cooked with a cold mayonnaise. It goes well with all kinds of dishes and is very refreshing.

Can serve up to 8 people.

Ingredients

  • 2 Carrots
  • 4 Potatoes
  • 1 Chayote
  • 1 Apple
  • 250g of mayonnaise
  • Oregano(optional)

Steps

  1. Pour 1000ml of water into a pan and bring to the boil;
  2. While the water boils, wash, peel and chop the vegetables and the apple;
  3. Once the water has boiled, add the chopped carrots;
  4. After 5 minutes, add the chopped chayote into the boiled water;
  5. After more 5 minutes, add the chopped potatoes;
  6. After 8 minutes, turn the stove off;
  7. Wait until de vegetables have cooled down;
  8. Place the vegetables and chopped apple in a bowl and mix with the desired amount of mayonnaise;
  9. Place in another bowl to serve at the table and put int the fridge;
  10. And finally, if you like, sprinkle a little oregano on the surface before serving the salad at the table.
