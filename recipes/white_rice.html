White Rice

Description

The white rice recipe is simple and classic. Rice is a versatile side dish and can be used in a variety of preparations.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 2 cups of boiled water
  • A half chopped onion
  • 1 cup of rice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Saute the garlic and onio in olive oil;
  2. Add the rice and fry for about 30 seconds;
  3. Add the boiling water and salt;
  4. Lower the heat and cook until the water is almost gone;
  5. Cover the pan and wait about 20 minutes before serving.
